\begin{spell}{Air Bubble}{Homebrew}{\nth{1} level abjuration (ritual)}
{
    \spTime{1 action}
    \spRange{Self (10 -oot radius)}
    \spComponents{V, S}
    \spDuration{1 hour}
}
When you cast this spell, everyone in a 10~foot radius
around you gets a bubble of air around their head. For the
duration of the spell, they have no trouble breathing, no
matter the environment.

\note{At Higher Levels.} When you cast this spell using a
spell slot of \nth{2}~level or higher, the duration of this
spell is increased by an additional hour for each slot
level above~\nth{1}.
\end{spell}
